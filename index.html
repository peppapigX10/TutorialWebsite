My newest book - Fable of the Unseen Guide

Book Blurb

Fantasy and mental health. | Katie, a thirteen-year-old female, exhausted and weak, finds out she possesses immense power. On a journey through self-hatred and friendship, while the police try to capture her, labelling her as a threat. A terrible disaster runs through the earth, making the world's end near, a disaster seemingly impossible to defeat. A group finds potential in those with power and seeks to earn their cooperation. As Katie learns more about herself, she discovers she is not the only one with power in this seemingly ordinary world.

Characters

  • Katie Fox
  • Jae-ho Leo
  • Jae-ho's grandpa
  • Mysterious man

This book contains recurring themes of mental health such as self-hatred, bullying, nightmares and more.
